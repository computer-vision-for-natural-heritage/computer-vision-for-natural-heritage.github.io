We invite paper submissions about unpublished work. The papers will go through a double-blind review and, if accepted, they will be published in the ECCV workshop proceedings. All accepted papers will be presented as a <b>poster</b> and as a two-minutes <b>pitch presentation</b>. Additionally, the most outstanding works will be presented as an <b>oral presentation</b>.

The covered topics include but are not limited to:

<ul>
<li>Digitization and mobilisation of specimen data from labels and archive cards</li>
<li>2D and 3D specimen imaging</li>
<li>Robotic vision for automated imaging</li>
<li>Species recognition and AI‑assisted species description</li>
<li>Phenology estimation from collection specimens</li>
<li>Benchmarks, datasets, and evaluation protocols</li>
<li>Vision-based quality control and error detection</li>
<li>Morphological trait extraction using CV</li>
<li>Computational challenges for species recognition (CT-scans of micro‑fossils and handwritten insect labels)</li>
</ul>
